Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Ready to make the best fudge of your life? You've come to the right place! You'll come back to this creamy fudge recipe again and again.

This is the best fudge recipe ever! It's my aunt's recipe, passed down through the family. It's easy enough to whip up in 15 minutes or so.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk (not condensed milk)
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 jar (7 oz) marshmallow creme/fluff
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pan: Line an 8x8-inch square baking dish with aluminum foil or parchment paper. Grease lightly with butter.
  2. Cook the Sugar Mixture: In a large saucepan, combine the sugar, butter, and evaporated milk. Stir constantly over medium heat until the mixture comes to a full rolling boil.
  3. Boil and Stir: Once boiling, continue stirring constantly for about 4-5 minutes. Use a candy thermometer if possible; the mixture should reach around 234°F (112°C).
  4. Add Chocolate and Marshmallow Creme: Remove the saucepan from heat. Stir in the chocolate chips, marshmallow creme, and vanilla extract. Mix well until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth.
  5. Optional – Add Nuts: If you're adding nuts, fold them into the mixture at this stage.
  6. Transfer and Cool: Pour the fudge mixture into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly. Let the fudge cool at room temperature for at least 2 hours, or refrigerate to speed up the process.
  7. Cut and Serve: Once fully set, remove the fudge from the pan by lifting the foil or parchment paper. Cut into squares and serve.
