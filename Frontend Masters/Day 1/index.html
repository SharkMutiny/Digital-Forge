Make a Peanut Butter and Jam Sandwich

  1. Get some peanut butter, jam (I like strawberry), and 2 pieces of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread and the jam on the other.
  3. Put the peanut butter and jam together, with the bread side facing out.
  4. Eat and Enjoy!
